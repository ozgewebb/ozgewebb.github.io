Chapter 1: Physical Characteristics of Pugs

Pugs have wrinkly, short-muzzled face, and curled tail. An adult pug usually weigh 14 to 18 pounds and grow to heights of 10 to 11 inches. Their ears are tiny and circular, and they have deep and brown eyes. Pugs come in different colors -fawn, black, apricot and silver.

They tend to weight gain and obesity, so their families must pay close attention to their feeding plans and get a moderate amount of exercise every day.

Chapter 2: Behavioral Characteristics of Pugs

They are happy and very friendly! Pugs are smart dogs and so funny! They are the best dogs ever!
